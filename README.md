# Vibe Coding the Game of War

Thinking what a good first vibe coding project could be, I thought of war. Easy known mechanics, and hopefully not too hard to do.

Time will tell what will happen.

---

## Play the game

Open `index.html` in your browser (double-click or drag into a browser window). No build step required.

Standard War rules: you and the computer each get 26 cards. Click **Battle** to reveal the top card; higher card wins the round. On a tie, it’s War—three face-down, then one face-up; higher face-up wins the whole pot.
